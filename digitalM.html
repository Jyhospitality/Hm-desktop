About Hospitality Minds

Hospitality Minds is a Hotel Marketing company initiated in venturing into the mid-market segment hotels in the Indian hospitality perspective.Hospitality Minds Being one of the Top 10 Hospitality consulting firms in India today offers its services to major hotels across the country in various destinations like Mumbai, Darjeeling, Gangtok, Igatpuri, Shirdi, Mahabaleshwar, Gurgoan, Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Goa.

Role

Digital Marketing Intern

Job Location

Mumbai / WFH

Core Job Responsibilities and & Duties

  • Handling social media pages of hotels.
  • Thinking about creatives of the hotels and getting it done.
  • Growing the page popularity.
  • Posting the banners on pages and making them popular.
  • Boosting the page/post as per client request.
  • Preparing social media reports and sending them to the clients.
  • Growing the online reputation of clients.
  • Coordinating with graphic designers and giving them ideas.
  • Visiting hotels once in a while.
  • Optimizing visibility on Google.
  • Engaging in Google marketing.
  • Replying to online reviews of the hotels.
  • Handling the brand reputation of the hotel.
  • Working on the SEO of the hotels.
