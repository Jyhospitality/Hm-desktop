Business Development Executive - Job Description

About Hospitality Minds

Hospitality Minds is a Hotel Marketing company initiated in venturing into the mid-market segment hotels in the Indian hospitality perspective. Hospitality Minds being one of the top 10 hospitality consulting firms in India today offers its services to major hotels across the country in various destinations like Mumbai, Darjeeling, Gangtok, Igatpuri, Shirdi, Mahabaleshwar, Gurgoan, Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Goa.

Role

Business Development Executive

Job Location

Mumbai

Skills Required:

  • Must have good verbal and written communication skills.
  • Should be responsive to calls whenever it comes
  • Must be flexible to travel to any part of India.
  • Must have good persuasive skills.
  • Organized
  • Presentable.
  • Soft Skills

Core Job Responsibilities and & Duties

  • Work under CEO of the company.
  • Perform market research of hotels.
  • Identify the hotels that need our services.
  • Arrange for meeting via call.
  • Handle the hotel setup phase along with other team members.
  • Provide quality suggestions to hotels on how to improve their business.
  • Make proposals / deals to hotels.
